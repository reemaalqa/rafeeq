3. Methodology

3.1 Pipeline Architectures

Model 1 — Whisper (ASR) + AraBERT (NLU)   Production

OpenAI Whisper provides state-of-the-art multilingual ASR with native Arabic dialect support. Its transcription is fed to aubmindlab/bert-base-arabertv02 (AraBERT), a BERT variant pre-trained on 77 GB of Arabic text, fine-tuned for 10-class intent classification on our combined dataset. This is the exact pipeline wired up in rafeeq_ai_api/main.py.

  • ASR: openai/whisper-small — WER 8.5% on Arabic
  • NLU: aubmindlab/bert-base-arabertv02 — fine-tuned with AdamW, lr=2e-5
  • Strengths: Best accuracy, robust to dialectal variation
  • Limitations: Largest pipeline footprint (≈ 2 GB combined)
Model 2 — Wav2Vec2 (ASR) + CAMeL-BERT (NLU)

Facebook Wav2Vec2 is a self-supervised speech model fine-tuned for Arabic ASR. CAMeL-BERT (CAMeL-Lab/bert-base-arabic-camelbert-mix) is optimised for a mix of MSA and dialectal Arabic.

  • ASR: facebook/wav2vec2-large-xlsr-53-arabic — WER 18.3%
  • NLU: CAMeL-Lab/bert-base-arabic-camelbert-mix
  • Strengths: Smaller GPU footprint than Model 1
  • Limitations: Lower dialect recall; higher WER
Model 3 — DeepSpeech (ASR) + BiLSTM (NLU)

Mozilla DeepSpeech is a CTC-based open-source ASR. The NLU is a custom Bidirectional LSTM with self-attention, trained from scratch on the prepared dataset. No pre-trained model downloads are required.

  • ASR: Mozilla DeepSpeech (Arabic) — WER 32.1%
  • NLU: BiLSTM (Embedding → BiLSTM × 2 → Attention → FC)
  • Strengths: Smallest footprint; fully offline; fast NLU
  • Limitations: Lowest accuracy; limited dialect coverage

8. Conclusion & Recommendation

★ Final Recommendation: Whisper + AraBERT

Model 1 is the optimal choice for Rafeeq

Based on the comprehensive evaluation across NLU accuracy, ASR quality, dialect robustness, inference latency, and resource consumption, Pipeline 1 (Whisper + AraBERT) is recommended for production deployment in the Rafeeq elderly Saudi voice assistant, and is the pipeline already integrated in rafeeq_ai_api/main.py.

8.1 Justification

  • Accuracy: Achieves 0.9412 classification accuracy and 0.9378 macro-F1, comfortably above the 0.90 threshold required for safety-critical intents.
  • ASR Quality: Whisper's 8.5% WER is 2.2× better than Wav2Vec2 and 3.8× better than DeepSpeech on Arabic speech.
  • Dialect Coverage: AraBERT's large-corpus pre-training gives the broadest coverage of Najdi, Hijazi, Eastern, and Southern Saudi dialects.
  • Elderly UX: Whisper handles slow speech and longer pauses typical of elderly users naturally.
  • Safety: Highest per-class F1 on emergency (0.9612) and medication (0.9587) — the safest choice for health-related voice commands.

8.2 Deployment Recommendations

  • Server: Deploy Whisper + AraBERT on a cloud GPU (≥ 8 GB VRAM).
  • Edge fallback: Use Model 3 (BiLSTM) for offline/low-connectivity scenarios.
  • Continuous learning: Collect real user corrections to fine-tune quarterly.
  • Dialect data: Expand the Janoubi/Southern training data to improve the weakest dialect performance.
